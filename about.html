About Us

Welcome to the page containing extra information about Verci High School. Discover what makes Verci, the school where learning meets exciting possibilities, special.

Verci was established by founders Thiana Desabille, Corinne Roldan, and Zaki Leaño, following the catastrophic Covid apocalypse in 2023. It originally started as a refugee camp that grew in size and complexity when survivors from all over the country and certain parts of the world settled in the safe haven.

Years after its founding, the refugee camp became a peaceful commune and the inhabitants started raising families of their own. It was collectively decided that the Verci commune should be turned into Verci Public High School in order to give the children proper education and structure in a post-apocalyptic world.

Ceres Sirena Cerante - Campus director
Madam Cessy is a 3rd generation member of unknown orign. She arrived unannounced on one stormy night, seemingly materializing out of the shadows. Whispers circulated regarding Madam Cessy's background, hinting at a remarkable life veiled in mystery. Some asserted she had served as an intelligence operative in the past, while others maintained that she possessed age-old wisdom handed down through generations. Despite the varying conjectures, one indisputable fact remained — her arrival cast a captivating aura over Verci High.

Despite her infamous reputation, all the students at Verci High deeply appreciate her. Her outgoing and friendly personality creates a positive and joyful atmosphere within the school. Her ability to bring people together transcends any negative comments about her. In essence, she plays a crucial role in fostering unity and camaraderie among the student body.

Rick Provec Gregorio - Student Services Division
Sir Greg is a 2nd generation Verci resident, one that used to teach in the Mathematics unit before shifting to the Student Services Division after the 2050 outbreak, which caused the untimely deaths of several division members. He is the oldest living Verci High employee.

Sir Greg doesn't appreciate conversations about his age, and it remains a mystery as nobody is privy to his actual age.

Clarisse Fidelis Manalang - Head of School Resources
Maam La is a 4th generation Verci alumna whose parents came during the wave of foreign refugees. Maam La was the salutatorian of Batch 2108. After graduating, Ma’am La became part of the English unit before shifting to the Guidance Unit where she served for 3 years. After which, she became an administrator and eventually, the Head of School Resources.

Maam La is quite popular around the school. She tends to visit the younger years to give them treats, the treats being extra homework.. and candy.

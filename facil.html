Facilities

Verci highschool's facilities are tailored to the needs and wants of the students and the overall message of the school. Verci is dedicated to ensuring the well-roundedness and capability of all of its students in all aspects in life. The founders and succeeding school officials have personally built many facilities in order to better conduct learning.

Some of the presently available facilities are:

BIOLOGY LABORATORY

Due to the ongoing apocalypse, Verci provides students with a Biology Laboratory to better understand the workings of the human body and maybe someday find a cure. The higher batches have made significant breakthroughs in achieving this goal. Lower batches focus on learning the human body in preparation for their higher years.

A lower batch conducted laboratory simulation can be found here.

LIBRARY

The library is full of many different types of media. From statistical studies on the projected duration of the apocalypse to student-contributed fanfiction, Verci highschool proudly displays the award "Most Books" as the only remaining highschool with intact books.
